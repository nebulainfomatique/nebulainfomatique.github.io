About Us

We are not about selling products. We are about solving problems.

We know that you would love to have a modern, efficient and eloquent work place where everything just works effortlessly, seamlessly and without being a head ache for your IT department. So, that you and your team can focus on propelling your business to new heights. But in this maze of never ending product launches and upcoming technologies it is harder than ever to achieve this goal of a perfect office.
So this is where we step in.
Nebula Infomatique are leading system integrators and office automation firm which have set new industry standards in providing efficient future-ready and cost effective solution for all your office needs. From planning to purchasing and till post-sales maintainance we are there for you.

 

Products

We at Nebula Infomatique are committed to provide our clients with a vast array of options from all the leading brands of the industry so that you can choose the best product at most competitive price and be cent percent sure of its authenticity and quality. We are the one stop for all your office needs dealing in communication systems, video-audio conferencing, data switches, biometric solutions, surveillance systems and a whole lot more.

Services

Our team has always tried to excel in the domain of post sales service because of our belief in the fact that once a product is sold that is when the deal ends and relationship begins. We take every step possible to make maintenance and post sales an easy and pleasant experience for you by providing considerate options such as annual and semi-annual maintenance contract, on call service, thorough installation to name a few.

The Best Brands

We are dealer partners of top brands and industry leaders ensuring that you get the best of the best products.
